Bio

My name is Yufeng GU. I’m a senior undergraduate student at Zhejiang University, P. R. China. I major in Information Engineering at College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering and minor in the Advanced Honor Class of Engineering Education (ACEE) in Chu Kochen Honors College.

Currently, I’m doing my Bachelor’s thesis internship on machine learning and biomedical imaging at Yale Univeristy, supervised by Prof. James Duncan. In summer 2019, I did internship in PARSA at EPFL, working on computer architecture and machine learning, supervised by Prof. Babak Falsafi. Before that, I also worked with Prof. Peng Liu at Zhejiang University on machine learning accelerators.

Research Interests

  • Computer architecture, machine learning acceleration and security.
  • Biomedical imaging and interpretale machine learning methods.
